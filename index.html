Cybersecurity Threats and Tips

The overall website will feature about 5 different types of cybersecurity threats. For each of those 5 threats, there will be short descriptions of what they are, how they occur. Also, some tips are given on how to prevent those types of threats from happening or lower your risk of becoming a victim . Then the user will be given a multiple choice quiz covering all the threats they have learned about. Answer key will be provided at the end of the quiz to show the correct answers.

A few common type of cyber security threats are:

Phishing
Malware Attack
Password Attack
DDoS attack
Man in the Middle Attack
