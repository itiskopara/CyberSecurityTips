Cybersecurity Threats and Tips

The overall website will feature about 5 different types of cybersecurity threats. For each of those 5 threats, there will be short descriptions of what they are, how they occur, real-world examples of what they are like, Also some tips on how to prevent those types of threats from happening . Then the user will be given a multiple choice quiz covering all the threats they have learned about. Answer key will be provided at the end of the quiz to show the correct answers.

A few common type of cyber security threats are:

Phishing
Malware attack
Password attack
DDoS attack
Man in the Middle
